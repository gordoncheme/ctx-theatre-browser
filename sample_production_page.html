Songs for a New World
by Waco Civic Theatre

Jan. 16 - Jan. 24, 2026
Fridays-Saturdays

Jason Robert Brown’s powerful and moving song cycle, Songs for a New World — a stunning collection of stories about life, love, and the moments that define us. From the deck of a Spanish sailing ship to the top of a New York penthouse, each song captures a pivotal “new world” moment — when decisions are made, dreams are tested, and hearts are laid bare. With unforgettable music and soul-stirring lyrics, this production is an incredible showcase for passionate, dynamic vocalists and storytellers.

CTX Live Theatre is a project — or maybe no more than a hobby, bordering on a quiet obsession — devoted to supporting live narrative theatre in Central Texas. It is maintained by Michael Meigs and fueled by the passion of the theatre community in the Austin–San Antonio area.
